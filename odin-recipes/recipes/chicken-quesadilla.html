Chicken Quesadilla

Chicken quesadilla recipe

This is a wonderful, easy to make recipe that will make you burst.

Ingredients and kitchen necessites

  • Pan or skillet
  • Spacthela
  • Knife
  • Flour tortillas
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked paprika
  • Cumin
  • 1 lb Chicken
  • Quesadilla melting cheese

Steps

  1. Season all sides of chicken with the following seasonings put as much as you like
  2. Turn stove on medium-high heat
  3. Half a Stick of butter on the pan
  4. Cook chicken
  5. Make sure chicken isn't raw, then set aside
  6. Turn heat down to low-medium heat
  7. butter the pan
  8. Slap that tortilla on
  9. Add how ever much cheese you want on tortilla
  10. Add your cooked chicken
  11. Once cheese has melted slap a tortilla on top
  12. Flip tortilla to the new one you just added
  13. Flip until you get a nice golden browining of the tortillas
  14. Thats it, slap that thing on a plate with suace of your choice and enjoy :)
